OREO MILKSHAKE RECIPE

June 17, 2020June 18, 2020 swathynandhini

Oreo milkshake recipe with step-wise pictures. Oreo milkshake is delicious, rich and creamy drink prepared with oreo cookies, ice cream and milk. This is super easy to make and will be ideal dessert drink for kids. This can be served with ice cream or whipped cream on top. 

You may also like similar recipes:

  • Bourbon Milkshake
  • Banana chocolate sharjah shake
  • Low fat mango milkshake
  • Dates milkshake
  • Tender coconut milkshake
  • Ragi milkshake


INGREDIENTS:

  • Oreo cookies – 15
  • Milk – 2 cups
  • Vanilla ice cream – 4 scoops
  • Instant coffee powder – 1/2 tsp (optional)

METHOD:

Crush roughly and add oreo cookies to a blender along with other ingredients. Blend together until frothy.

Take a tall glasses, pour some chocolate syrup over the sides. Pour the prepared oreo milkshake and refrigerate it. 

Before serving, add ice cream or whipped cream on top and garnish with some crushed oreo cookies. Serve immediately and enjoy.
